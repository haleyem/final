Fun Things to Do

In downtown Chicago, or The Loop and the adjacent neighborhoods, there are many free and low-cost cultural attractions. Some require planning ahead, since they are only free during certain days or hours.

Free All The Time

  • Chicago Cultural Center
  • Millenium Park & Jay Pritzker Pavilion
  • Walk the Riverwalk
