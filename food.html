Resturants

Breakfast

There are two breakfast places I really enjoy. The cheap option is Golden House Restaurant & Pancake House, which is a cash only diner. You can get a good breakfast there for less than $10. A mid-price option is Tweet, which serves American food and some Mexican food. Everything I've had there is delicious.

There are a few bakeries and coffee shops I enjoy if you prefer a smaller breakfast. Two good bakeries are La PÃ¢tisserie P and Chiu Quon Bakery. First Sip Cafe and Emerald City Coffee are two good coffee shops near those bakeries.

Lunch

Tweet is also open for lunch and dinner. Sun Wah BBQ is low cost and is well known for their duck. Beat Kitchen has good vegan and non-vegan options.
