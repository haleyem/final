Leisure

There are a variety of leisure activities in Chicago, and they vary by season. I prefer the summertime, but there are things to do all year round.

Summer

There are a lot of beaches in Chicago on the North and South sides of the city. The beaches closest to Downtown, like Oat Street Beach and North Avenue Beach, can be fun, but they're crowded. On the Northside, Hollywood Beach is a queer community hub. On the Southside, South Shore Beach has a beautiful view of the skyline and has the South Shore Cultural Center.

Winter

In the winter, there is a skating rink in Millenium Park.
